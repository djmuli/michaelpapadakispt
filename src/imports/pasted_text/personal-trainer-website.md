# Redesign this Personal Trainer Website

Update the existing website design using the content and images provided.

The goal is to create a premium, high-converting personal trainer website for Michael Papadakis PT that feels professional, trustworthy, experienced and results-focused.

Do not create a bodybuilding competition website.

This website is for everyday people looking to improve their fitness, lose weight, build strength, improve confidence and work with an experienced coach.

## Brand Positioning

Michael Papadakis is:

* Personal Trainer
* Fitness Coach
* Natural Bodybuilding Champion
* 40+ years training experience
* Based at World Gym Marrickville

The bodybuilding achievements should be used as credibility and proof of expertise, not as the primary focus of the website.

The primary message should be:

"Helping everyday people achieve lasting fitness results through personalised coaching."

## Design Direction

Create a modern premium fitness website.

Style:

* Black background
* Red accents
* White typography
* Strong contrast
* Clean and professional
* Luxury gym aesthetic
* Mobile-first design
* Similar quality to premium boutique fitness brands

Avoid:

* Cheap fitness templates
* Excessive animations
* Generic stock fitness imagery
* Overly aggressive bodybuilding style

## Image Usage

Prioritise coaching images and client interaction photos.

Use approximately:

* 80% coaching/client photos
* 20% bodybuilding champion photos

The bodybuilding images should appear in:

* About section
* Credentials section

The coaching photos should dominate:

* Hero
* Services
* Testimonials
* Call to action sections

Use the supplied logo throughout the design.
